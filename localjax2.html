Local Jax

Hello, Everyone! Local Jax is a website that strives to promote small businesses located in Jacksonville. Our mission is to shed light on the hardworking people of Jacksonville, FL. Supporting small businesses is critical to the success of Jacksonville as a whole. We will introduce you to local gems that you can support in our community!
